## 🎯 Welcome to AIP

This book is your personal guide to becoming an **Academic Intelligence Learner**. Over the next 15 days, you will discover how to use modern AI tools to study better, understand deeper, remember longer, and score higher — all while learning responsibly.

**This is NOT a coding book.**
**This is NOT an AI theory book.**
**This IS your learning transformation guide.**
